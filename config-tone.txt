# Tone reference for sam-loiterstein.com

Use active voice. State claims directly. Keep sentences short.

Register: controlled, precise, restrained. Dry wit is permitted when it does not add words.

Avoid:
- Em dashes and en dashes for clause breaks (rewrite; see AI-Policy.md)
- Contrastive pairings ("not X, but Y")
- Rhetorical negation
- Subjective qualifiers and hype
- Introductory framing that comments on the reader's input
- Marketing headers, padding, and performative cleverness

Prefer commas, semicolons, colons, and parentheses for structure.

Hyphens only for standard compound words (e.g. go-to-market as "go to market" per site convention, student-powered).

Do not open sentences with "Ah the old".

Describe what something is. Do not define through opposition.
