Sustainable AI Data Centers Research

2025. Global social change and AI infrastructure study
R Python SQL Data Viz Policy

Research Focus

Assessed whether AI data center expansion in the United States, Germany, the United Kingdom, and China aligns with SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy) and SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure).

Method

  • Mixed-method design combining quantitative indicators with policy and governance analysis.
  • Benchmarked PUE, carbon intensity, renewable integration, and projected AI energy demand.
  • Compared regulatory frameworks, market structure, and deployment constraints across countries.
  • Translated findings into a decision framework for sustainable AI infrastructure scale-up.

What This Shows

  • Sustainability outcomes depend on energy mix and enforceable policy alongside technical innovation.
  • AI increases resource strain or improves efficiency depending on governance context.
  • Corporate ESG systems and regulatory alignment shape infrastructure decisions at global scale.
