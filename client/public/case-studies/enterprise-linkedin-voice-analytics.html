Enterprise LinkedIn Voice + Analytics

2025. Multi-tenant LinkedIn publishing platform
Python R JavaScript TypeScript SQL

Overview

Built a multi-tenant enterprise platform for LinkedIn publishing across employee and corporate accounts. The system ingests company context, drafts posts with configurable voice controls, automates posting workflows, and reports performance across individual, team, and executive layers.

Core Value

  • Turns employees into consistent distribution channels.
  • Automates thought leadership while preserving author voice markers.
  • Gives measurable ROI visibility from post performance to campaign impact.
  • Balances centralized governance with individual voice control.

System Architecture

  • Data layer: Google Sheets, CSV, and API ingestion for brand context and messaging inputs.
  • AI content engine: layered context from biography, brand profile, voice fingerprint, and performance memory.
  • Topic intelligence: trend synthesis, insight extraction, and contrarian angle generation.
  • Posting automation: scheduled queues, best-time optimization, approvals, retries, and fallback delivery.
  • Analytics: individual, team, and sponsor dashboards with engagement and growth metrics.

AI Quality and Human Voice Controls

  • Prompt-level style enforcement to avoid predictable AI phrasing and cliches.
  • Voice fingerprinting from writing samples to preserve pacing and structural patterns.
  • Edit-distance and engagement feedback loops to improve draft quality over time.
  • Repetition, plagiarism, and tone consistency checks before publish.

Enterprise Controls

  • Role-based workflows for Admin, Manager, User, and Viewer roles.
  • Draft, review, approve, schedule, and auto-post options per team policy.
  • Multi-tenant branding configuration for domain, dashboard, and client-specific UI.
  • Integration-ready design for LinkedIn, OpenAI, Slack, HubSpot, and Salesforce.

Success Metrics

  • Engagement rate per post and follower growth rate.
  • Posts per user per week and content approval rate.
  • Theme-level performance and enterprise retention signals.
  • Attribution to pipeline and lead quality when CRM connections are enabled.
